What Is a Real Estate Agent?

A licensed real estate agent connects buyers and sellers for transactions and represents them in legal negotiations.
Generally, agents are compensated through commission, which is a percentage of the sale of the house.
The percentage agents receive depends on their state and brokerage.
A brokerage is the managing house for agents, allowing the agents to use the company branding, connections, and legal team.
